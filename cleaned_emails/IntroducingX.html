Where he comes from, everyone just calls him "X".  Nobody in town knows his real name, and you probably will not anytime soon.  He was an executioner, and a hulk of a man.  In terms of looks, he's basically Nathan Explosion from Dethklok:



He firmly believes that he is the instrument of Kelemvor, the Lord of the Dead and Judge of the Damned.  He became an adventurer partly due to a calling from Kelemvor, and partly because he was tired of waiting for the next scheduled execution, and wanted to be his own boss.  Judge and executioner.

His demeanor is mostly bored from the banality of life.  However, when you're doing what you love, you never work a day in your life.  His barbarian rage is less unbridled anger as it is a passionate desire.  The thrill of the hunt.  The reaper is inevitable.
