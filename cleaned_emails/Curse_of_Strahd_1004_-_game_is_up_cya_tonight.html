Regarding the poisoned condition, it's specific to the poison that is applied. In the case of this particular centipede swarm:

1. I presume you will make a check to figure out what's ailing you: this would be one of: Nature (Int), Survival (Wis) or Medicine (Wis). Given your diverse party abilities and current out-of-combat status, I will just handwave you passing this.
2. After reviewing the centipedes grilled corpses and your wounds you can tell the following about the poison:
 
You feel you will remain disadvantaged for about an hour. 
You also believe your body would naturally recover after: (a) a short rest, (b) a healing potion or spell, or (c) a lesser restoration spell.

From reading the players handbook you are confident taking a short rest would be some form of "recuperating".

Hope that helps, and if you think I missed anything in the core rules regarding this please let me know :)
