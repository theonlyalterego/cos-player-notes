AI augmented emails! This campaign is all about atmosphere and storytelling, with plenty of challenges along the way. Character Creation:

We’re sticking with the **Player’s Handbook** and the usual books we’ve agreed on (Xanathar’s, Tasha’s, etc.) for building your characters. I encourage you to think about how your character might react to the unexpected—whether they’re brave, cautious, or somewhere in between.

Also, consider picking a campaign trait that ties your character to the darker, mysterious themes of the adventure. Whether it’s a shadowy past, an unresolved mystery, or a fear they’re trying to overcome, these traits will help your character fit right into the story.

Meet the Party

  • Friar Gregor Blackthorn: A devout yet troubled cleric, Friar Gregor Blackthorn seeks redemption through his relentless pursuit of the monstrous evils that haunt his family’s legacy.

  • Francis: Francis now roams the land as a hulking barbarian, driven by the thrill of the hunt and the whispers of a dark god urging him toward greater conquests.

  • Rachel: A talented halfling portrait painter with a bright smile and friendly charm, Rachel’s happy demeanor cleverly masks a roguish edge, hinting at a knack for navigating life's shadows when needed.

I'll let you guess who's playing whom. Though you're free to talk amongst yourselves.

Next Steps:

1. Character Ideas: Start thinking about who you want to play. If you want to chat about your character concept or need some inspiration, feel free to reach out! I have a few characters already but not everyone ...
